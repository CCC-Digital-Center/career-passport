California Career Passport

The public home for the California Career Passport initiative, a learner-controlled digital credential wallet and the open infrastructure behind it, built on W3C Verifiable Credentials and open standards.

What This Is

The Career Passport gives Californians a secure, learner-controlled tool to hold and share verifiable academic records, licenses, industry credentials, badges, and military service, helping employers see the full picture of what someone knows and can do, and opening opportunity to workers whose skills extend beyond any single credential.

The wallet is the visible layer. The core work is the trust infrastructure underneath: open standards, interoperability, and a credential schema designed for portability across systems and is not the work of a single vendor or platform.
