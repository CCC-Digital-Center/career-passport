California Career Passport

The public home for the California Career Passport initiative, an open standards based digital credential wallet and the infrastructure behind it.

What is the Career Passport?

The Career Passport gives Californians a secure, learner-controlled tool to hold and share verifiable academic records, licenses, industry credentials, badges, and military service, helping employers see the full picture of what someone knows and can do, and opening opportunity to workers whose skills extend beyond any single credential.

The wallet is the visible layer. The core work is the trust infrastructure underneath and the open ecosystem it exists within. The architectural decisions emphsize open standards based interoperability and a foundational commitment to privacy and accessibility. This project is not the work of a single vendor or platform.
